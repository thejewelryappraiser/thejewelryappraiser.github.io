Marco Boetti holds Masters in Physics from the University of Torino and joined our laboratory as an Early Stage Researcher (ESR) under COMPLETE ITN consortium. 
Marco works with numerical and experimental methods on the problem of Lagrangian particles crossing turbulent interfaces in stratified fluids.
